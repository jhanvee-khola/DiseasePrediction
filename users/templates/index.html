PREDICT YOUR CHANCES OF HAVING A DISEASE


Due to modern lifestyle, diseases are increasing at an alarming rate. Our lifestyle, work type and food habits are the various factors which influence our health causing heart diseases and other health issues like Hypertension, Diabetes etc. In emerging countries like India, the urban population faces the wrath of it with 5.8 million deaths per year.
Data science and AI systems can be used for prediction and detection of such lethal diseases which will in turn help to take early actions against them.
